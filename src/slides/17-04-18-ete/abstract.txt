Saving Black Friday: Clojure to the Rescue

Two years ago modnakasta.ua already was one of the largest ecommerce platforms
in Ukraine.  Built in Python/Django it couldn't handle any spikes in traffic and
was hard to maintain and improve. How do you change a platform on the fly? A
tale of Clojure and ClojureScript, its incredible ecosystem and a crazy team
which brought bleeding edge technologies on the screens of hundreds of thousands
of people.
